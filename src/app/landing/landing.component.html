Fitness Trend
Train yourself properly by tracking your fitness, fatigue & form over time. Avoid training overload or injuries and get ready for your D-Days and races!
  • Supports all sports. Ready for triathletes with swim support.
  • Works without sensors: case you're smartphone user.
  • Supports dated athlete settings. Namely the evolution of your physiological attributes over time (heart rate, weight, power, ...).
Year Progressions
Track your progress over the year and compare with previous years.
  • Filter by type of activities and years.
  • Filter commutes and indoor training.
  • Progressions' types:
    • Distance.
    • Time.
    • Elevation gain.
    • Activity count.

Download
To install the "Elevate for Strava" extension into your Opera browser, please follow below steps:
  • First install the official "Chrome Extension Installer for Opera" by Opera here.
  • Then go to "Elevate for Strava" chrome store link here, then click "Add to Opera".
  • You will be prompted to go to Opera Extension Manager to validate the extension installation. Click "Ok" to proceed.
  • Next confirm the installation of "Elevate for Strava" in the Opera Extension Manager.
  • You're done. "Elevate for Strava" is now installed into your Opera browser and you will receive extension updates.
Note: You can remove the "Chrome Extension Installer" extension if you want.
Firefox version is coming soon... Extension is currently experimental on this browser and needs to be stabilized before any official release. Stay tuned.
